What's inside

Built for every part
of your event.

📊
Overview
Live status dashboard with countdown, budget summary, milestone progress, and team contacts at a glance.
💰
Budget
Track dollars and hours in one place. Line-item tables, bar charts, overage alerts, and CSV export.
📅
Timeline
Pre-event milestones, day-of schedule, and multi-day itineraries. Toggle between visual timeline and table view.
✅
Tasks
Assign tasks with due dates, priorities, and categories. Switch between list view and a full Kanban board.
📍
Venues
Multi-venue tabs with room blocks, BEO & F&B, contacts, budget breakdown, and contract notes.
🎬
AV Production
Equipment orders, tech staff assignments, load-in schedules, and a full run-of-show per room.
🤝
Sponsors
Track tiers, contracts, deliverable checklists, revenue totals, and communication history per sponsor.
👕
Merch
Inventory with low-stock alerts, giveaway tracker for general and pre-assigned distribution, vendor info.
🎟️
Registration
Attendee list with RSVP, check-in, dietary tracking, capacity bar, bulk CSV import and export.
